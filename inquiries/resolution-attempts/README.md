# Resolution Attempts

**Status:** DISCUSSION_DRAFT  
**Canonical status:** Non-canonical  
**Purpose:** Archive of proposed RABA responses to open problems  

## Purpose

This directory records attempts to resolve open RABA inquiry questions.

A resolution attempt may become:

- a concept draft;
- a governance draft;
- a checklist;
- a mapping document;
- a decision log entry;
- a rejected or deferred idea.

## Boundary

A resolution attempt is not a final answer.

It is a structured proposal that remains non-canonical until Human Owner approval.

## Handling Rule

For each resolution attempt, record:

- related inquiry;
- proposed approach;
- supporting legal / standards sources;
- supporting research notes;
- supporting external inputs;
- risks and limitations;
- decision status;
- next step.

## Adoption Rule

A resolution attempt becomes accepted only through explicit Human Owner confirmation.

Multi-AI agreement, expert agreement or apparent conceptual elegance is not approval.
