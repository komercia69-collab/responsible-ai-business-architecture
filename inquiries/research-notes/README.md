# Research Notes

**Status:** DISCUSSION_DRAFT  
**Canonical status:** Non-canonical  
**Purpose:** Source archive for scientific and analytical material relevant to RABA inquiries  

## Purpose

This directory records scientific publications, analytical papers, research summaries and expert reports relevant to RABA open problems.

Examples:

- AI agent governance papers;
- accountability frameworks;
- human oversight research;
- agentic AI risk studies;
- AI and law papers;
- human-AI interaction research;
- organizational AI adoption research.

## Boundary

Research sources inform RABA.

They do not automatically define RABA architecture.

Research conclusions may be contested, evolving or jurisdiction-specific. RABA interpretation must remain separate from the original source.

## Source Handling Rule

For each research note, record:

- title;
- author / institution;
- date;
- source link or citation;
- research question;
- key findings;
- relevance to RABA;
- limitations;
- RABA interpretation status.

## Important Note

This directory is for research memory and synthesis.

It is not a canonical claims database unless the Human Owner explicitly approves a specific interpretation.
