# Legal and Standards Sources

**Status:** DISCUSSION_DRAFT  
**Canonical status:** Non-canonical  
**Purpose:** Source archive for laws, standards and official guidance relevant to RABA inquiries  

## Purpose

This directory records legal, regulatory, standards and official guidance sources that are relevant to RABA open problems.

Examples:

- EU AI Act;
- GDPR;
- Product Liability Directive;
- ISO/IEC 42001;
- ISO/IEC 23894;
- ISO/IEC 42005;
- NIST AI RMF;
- official regulator guidance;
- national legislation relevant to AI agents.

## Boundary

Legal and standards sources are authoritative in their own domain, but their presence in this directory does not automatically make any RABA interpretation canonical.

RABA interpretation requires separate analysis and Human Owner approval.

## Source Handling Rule

For each source, record:

- source name;
- jurisdiction or issuing body;
- source type;
- date / version;
- relevant provisions;
- related RABA inquiry;
- RABA interpretation status;
- whether legal review is required.

## Important Note

This directory is not legal advice.

It is a structured source memory for responsible architecture development.
