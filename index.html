Responsible AI Business Architecture

A practical framework for integrating AI into business processes without losing human responsibility, transparency, auditability, or operational control.

AI may be probabilistic. Responsibility must not be.

Start with one real business process. Use the Quick Check, pilot intake form, canvas, and decision report to decide whether the process is ready for responsible AI support.
