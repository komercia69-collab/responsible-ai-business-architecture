## Meaning of Yellow signal

A Yellow signal means:

The pilot may proceed only under controlled conditions.

The organization should not treat the AI assistant as a reliable decision-maker.  
It should be introduced as a support tool with clear human oversight, restricted permissions, and documented review points.
