## Main Risk Signals

- AI is expected to influence financial outcomes.
- Human approval is not mandatory.
- Accountability boundaries are unclear.
- Rollback procedures are undefined.
- Fraud review is inconsistent.
- Customer communication may become autonomous.
- The process is not sufficiently standardized.

---

## Governance Interpretation

This use case combines probabilistic analysis with operational execution and customer impact.

The organization currently lacks sufficient governance maturity to support safe autonomous execution.

---

## Recommended Direction

Do not deploy as an autonomous refund system.

Instead:

- reduce AI authority;
- introduce mandatory confirmation gates;
- improve auditability;
- standardize escalation rules;
- define rollback procedures;
- redesign the pilot as recommendation-only.
