# Standards Mapping

## Responsible AI Business Architecture

> Governance architecture becomes stronger when connected to established operational and regulatory frameworks.

---

# Purpose

This document maps Responsible AI Business Architecture concepts to existing governance, compliance, security, and operational standards.

The objective is not replacing established standards.

The objective is providing an operational governance layer focused on controllable AI-enabled organizations.

---

# Core Principle

Most existing standards define what organizations should manage.

This framework focuses on how organizations remain operationally controllable while AI autonomy scales.
