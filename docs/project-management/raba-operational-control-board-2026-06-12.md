## 5. Capability-Frontier Handling Chain

Capability-frontier signals must follow this handling chain before any status-affecting use:

> Capability signal  
> → External Signal Log  
> → Provider Signal Translation Rule  
> → RABA boundary mapping  
> → Decision Log Queue if status-affecting  
> → Human Owner decision

Capability-frontier signals should be prioritized by:

- recency;
- source quality;
- public shareability;
- relevance to RABA boundaries;
- clarity of source and context;
- evidence of real deployment relevance;
- risk of public-use, endorsement, validation, adoption, partnership, compliance, implementation, or provider-facing overreach.

### Capability / governance distinction

A new AI capability does not mean an organization is ready to use that capability responsibly.

Capability expansion is not governance readiness.
