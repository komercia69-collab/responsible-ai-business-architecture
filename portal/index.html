The architecture problem

AI deployment is scaling faster than governance capability.

The real risk is not only model error. The larger enterprise risk is loss of operational controllability across agents, prompts, tools, data access and delegated workflows.

Invisible AI drift

AI-enabled operations gradually diverge from intended policy, owner intent and documented controls.

Connector misuse

MCP and external tools expand the blast radius from text generation into real operational actions.

Accountability gaps

When autonomous systems act, organizations must still know who approved, who monitored and who can intervene.

Governable Autonomy

The goal is not to block autonomy. The goal is to make autonomy observable, correctable and accountable.
