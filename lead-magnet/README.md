# Lead Magnet: AI Readiness Quick Check

This folder contains a practical entry point for people and organizations who want to understand the value of Responsible AI Business Architecture quickly.

## Main Lead Magnet

[AI Readiness Quick Check](ai-readiness-quick-check.md)

A 15-minute self-assessment that helps a person, team, or organization evaluate whether a business process is ready for a responsible AI pilot.

## Why This Works as a Lead Magnet

The quick check gives immediate value before asking for anything in return.

It helps the reader:

- identify a promising AI pilot candidate;
- understand whether the current process is ready;
- see where human control is required;
- recognize risk before automation;
- connect business value with responsibility;
- understand why the full framework may be useful.

## Target Reader

This lead magnet is designed for:

- business owners;
- CTOs;
- DPOs;
- consultants;
- AI adoption teams;
- operations managers;
- transformation leaders;
- organizations considering AI agents or AI automation.
