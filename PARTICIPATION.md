# Participation

Responsible AI Business Architecture is an early-stage practical framework for helping organizations design AI adoption with clear responsibility, human control, auditability, and measurable business value.

This page explains how different people and organizations can participate.

## Why Participate?

Many organizations want to use AI, but they lack a structured way to decide:

- which process is ready for AI;
- where AI may create value;
- where AI should only support or recommend;
- where human approval must remain mandatory;
- what data and permission boundaries are needed;
- how to run a safe first pilot;
- how to scale without losing control.

This project aims to become a practical bridge between business process diagnosis, responsible AI architecture, compliance concerns, and implementation.

## Participation Tracks

There are four main ways to participate:

1. Review the framework.
2. Provide a real pilot environment.
3. Help implement or validate the approach.
4. Support productization and scaling.

## 2. Pilot Partners

Pilot partners bring a real business process that can be evaluated using the framework.

### Good Fit For

- small and medium-sized businesses;
- service companies;
- customer support teams;
- accounting and finance teams;
- operations teams;
- internal knowledge management teams;
- nonprofits;
- local institutions;
- organizations considering a first AI pilot.

### Good Pilot Processes

Good first pilot candidates are usually:

- limited in scope;
- repetitive;
- measurable;
- useful for real employees;
- low or medium risk;
- reversible;
- connected to visible business pain;
- designed with human review.

Examples:

- customer email triage;
- invoice data extraction;
- support ticket classification;
- internal knowledge search;
- weekly report drafting;
- document summarization.

### What Pilot Partners Gain

Pilot partners may gain:

- structured review of one AI idea;
- clearer understanding of risks and controls;
- a responsible pilot concept;
- a decision report;
- better communication between business, IT, and compliance;
- early influence on the framework based on real-world needs.

### Suggested First Action

Start with:

- [AI Readiness Quick Check](QUICK-CHECK.md)
- [Pilot Partner Call](offers/pilot-partner-call.md)

[AI Use Case Review](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/ai-use-case-review.md)

## Important Boundaries

This framework is not legal advice.

It does not guarantee GDPR compliance, EU AI Act compliance, security compliance, or business success.

It helps organizations ask better questions and design safer pilots before making legal, technical, compliance, and investment decisions.

## Key Message

> Responsible AI becomes valuable when it can be tested in real processes without losing human responsibility.

Participation helps turn the framework from a concept into a practical, validated, and scalable methodology.
